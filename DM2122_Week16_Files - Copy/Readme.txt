Controls:

W-Move Forward
A-Move Left
S-Move Back
D-Move Right

Arrow Up - Turn up
Arrow Down - Turn down
Arrow Left - Turn upleft
Arrow Right - Turn right

Spacebar - to Jump

Z-First personcamera
X-Free moving camera(charactor wont move at that stage, can see the animation of moving)
C-3rd Person Camera

J-ChangeLight

1-EnableCulling
2-DisableCulling
3-Fill mode
4-Wireframe mode
